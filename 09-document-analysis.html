Effective Date: March 15, 2021

SECTION 7: TERM AND TERMINATION

7.1 Term. This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue in effect until the expiration of the last to expire Royalty Term, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Section 7.

7.2 Termination for Breach. Either Party may terminate this Agreement upon written notice to the other Party if such other Party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within sixty (60) days after receipt of written notice thereof.

7.3 Change of Control. In the event of a Change of Control of Licensee, this Agreement and all rights and licenses granted hereunder shall automatically terminate unless Licensor provides its prior written consent to such Change of Control, which consent may be withheld in Licensor's sole discretion1. For purposes of this Section, "Change of Control" means (a) the acquisition by any person or entity of beneficial ownership of more than fifty percent (50%) of the voting power of the outstanding securities of Licensee; or (b) a merger, consolidation, or similar transaction.

7.4 Competing Acquirer. Notwithstanding Section 7.3, in the event of a Change of Control in which the acquiring party is listed on Exhibit B (Competing Companies), Licensor may terminate this Agreement immediately without liability upon written notice2.

7.5 Termination for Convenience. Licensee may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon ninety (90) days' prior written notice to Licensor3.

7.6 Effect of Termination. Upon termination of this Agreement for any reason:

(a) all rights and licenses granted to Licensee hereunder shall immediately terminate;

(b) Licensee shall return or destroy all Confidential Information of Licensor;

(c) Licensee shall cease all development, manufacture, and commercialization of Licensed Products within thirty (30) days4.
